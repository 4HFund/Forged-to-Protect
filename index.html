About the Event

More Than an Event.
A Community Statement.

Forged to Protect is a chance for families, kids, churches, businesses, veterans, and local agencies to come together and say thank you to the people who show up when our community needs them most.

We believe in honoring service — military and civilian alike — not just with words but with presence, recognition, and real community celebration.
